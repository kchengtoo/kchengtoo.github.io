Data Aggregation.

Data aggregation is the initiative to centralize multiple enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems by integrating them with the Digital Commercial Bank. This allows clients to access all their financial information in one place and opens up free time to focus on strategy analysis and forecasting.

I was in charge of the ERP integration enrollment and the marketplace to introduce the ERPs available to connect to the Digital Commercial Bank.

    User & Business Needs

    User: One place for all data to live where they can access it
    Business: Gather data to provide product recommendation

    Impact

    Shipped an MVP. Integration enrollment increased by 40% and an average of 8 ERP systems integrated per client.

    Contribution

    Lead designer for ERP integration enrollment and marketplace in collaboration with PMs and engineers

Problem

Startup clients use ERP systems to help streamline financial processes and help forecast business growth. However, using multiple ERPs may end up making it difficult for clients to process information and plan for strategies. With Data Aggregation, it addresses the issue by connecting client's ERPs to the Digital Commercial Bank, and my job is to design the experience for the enrollment of connecting the ERP systems and the marketplace where clients can find integrable ERPs that meet their business needs.

Challenge: How might we help clients better introduce ERP systems for their business needs and streamline the integration process?
